Frequently Asked Questions

How do I withdraw my earnings?
You can withdraw your cash straight to your PayPal account once you reach the minimum payout threshold of $10. Transfers usually process within 1-3 business days.
I haven't received credit for a game.
Please ensure you permitted tracking when downloading the app, and that you are a new user to that specific game. If you met the requirements, credit should appear within 24-48 hours.
Why was my survey disqualified?
Surveys look for very specific demographics (e.g., age, profession) and end early if you do not fit the target audience. Don't worry, there are always more surveys available in your dashboard.
